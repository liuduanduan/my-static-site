# 走火入魔 · Qi Deviation (Zǒuhuǒ Rùmó)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 修炼出偏差、心神失守或真元暴走造成的危险状态。

**English:** A dangerous failure in cultivation control, often involving unstable Qi and mind.

## 定义与概述 · Definition

**中文：** 走火入魔（Zǒuhuǒ Rùmó）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。修炼出偏差、心神失守或真元暴走造成的危险状态。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

**English:** Qi Deviation is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A dangerous failure in cultivation control, often involving unstable Qi and mind. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

## 在修炼中的作用 · Role in Cultivation

**中文：** 走火入魔让修炼具有代价，提醒读者心境与方法同样重要。

**English:** Qi deviation gives cultivation a cost and makes mental stability matter.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.
