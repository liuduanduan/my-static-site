## Definition

Qi is the ambient spiritual energy that cultivators absorb, circulate, refine, store, and transform into power.

## Detailed Explanation

Qi may exist in the air, spirit stones, herbs, secret realms, dragon veins, or heavenly treasures. A place rich in qi is often valuable territory for sects. Early cultivation usually begins by sensing qi and drawing it into the body.
