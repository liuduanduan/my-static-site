# 内丹 · Inner Alchemy (Nèidān)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 道教修炼中以内在身心为炉鼎、炼精化气的理论体系。

**English:** A Taoist framework that treats the body and mind as the furnace for inner refinement.

## 定义与概述 · Definition

**中文：** 内丹（Nèidān）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。道教修炼中以内在身心为炉鼎、炼精化气的理论体系。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

**English:** Inner Alchemy is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A Taoist framework that treats the body and mind as the furnace for inner refinement. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

## 在修炼中的作用 · Role in Cultivation

**中文：** 内丹术为金丹、元婴、炼气化神等修仙语汇提供文化根基。

**English:** Inner alchemy supplies cultural roots for Golden Core, Nascent Soul, and refinement language.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.
