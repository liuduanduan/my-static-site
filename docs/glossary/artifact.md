# 法宝 · Artifact / Dharma Treasure (Fǎbǎo)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 修仙者炼制或驱使的法器、灵宝和战斗工具。

**English:** A refined magical treasure, weapon, or tool used by cultivators.

## 定义与概述 · Definition

**中文：** 法宝（Fǎbǎo）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。修仙者炼制或驱使的法器、灵宝和战斗工具。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

**English:** Artifact / Dharma Treasure is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A refined magical treasure, weapon, or tool used by cultivators. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

## 在修炼中的作用 · Role in Cultivation

**中文：** 法宝既是战力来源，也是机缘、传承和身份象征。

**English:** Artifacts express power, inheritance, opportunity, and personal style.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.
