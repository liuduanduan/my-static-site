## Definition

An artifact is a supernatural tool or treasure created, refined, inherited, or discovered by cultivators.

## Detailed Explanation

Artifacts can be swords, cauldrons, bells, seals, flags, gourds, robes, rings, boats, or formations in object form. Powerful artifacts may have spirits, lineage restrictions, or histories tied to ancient powers.
