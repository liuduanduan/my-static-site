# 飞剑 · Flying Sword (Fēijiàn)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 可御使飞行、攻击或代步的剑类法器。

**English:** A sword artifact used for flight, attack, or travel.

## 定义与概述 · Definition

**中文：** 飞剑（Fēijiàn）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。可御使飞行、攻击或代步的剑类法器。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

**English:** Flying Sword is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A sword artifact used for flight, attack, or travel. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

## 在修炼中的作用 · Role in Cultivation

**中文：** 飞剑是仙侠视觉符号之一，连接御剑飞行、剑修和本命法宝。

**English:** Flying swords are iconic xianxia imagery, linking travel, sword cultivation, and artifacts.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.
