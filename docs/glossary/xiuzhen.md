# 修真 · Xiuzhen (Xiūzhēn)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 学道修行、求得真我、去伪存真的修持方式。

**English:** A Dao-oriented practice of cultivating truth and returning to the genuine self.

## 定义与概述 · Definition

**中文：** 修真（Xiūzhēn）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。学道修行、求得真我、去伪存真的修持方式。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

**English:** Xiuzhen is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A Dao-oriented practice of cultivating truth and returning to the genuine self. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

## 在修炼中的作用 · Role in Cultivation

**中文：** 修真常比修仙更强调心性、悟道和内在真实，是修仙叙事的哲学底色。

**English:** Xiuzhen often emphasizes temperament, Dao comprehension, and inner truth.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.
