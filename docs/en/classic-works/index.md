## Genre Branches

- Mortal flow: slow progression, resource scarcity, careful survival
- Primordial myth flow: ancient gods, Honghuang cosmology, mythic lineages
- Infinite or world-hopping flow: cultivation mixed with multiple worlds or missions
- Sect competition: disciples, tournaments, inheritances, and political rivalries
