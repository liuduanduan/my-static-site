## Summary

The last master of True Word Sect, a time-law cultivator around late Great Luo, whose Great Five Elements Illusory World Art lineage is inherited by Han Li across time.

## Role in RMJI

He is central for understanding the fall of True Word Sect, Qi Mozi betrayal, Gu Huojin pressure, and Han Li rebuilding the sect.
