# Timeline / 剧情脉络

A staged route tracking Han Li age band, location, gains, and key events.


## Age Calibration

| Realm | Estimated Han Li Age | Calibration Points |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mortal Realm | about age 10-1007 | Age 10 enters Qixuanmen; 21 Blood Forbidden Land; 31 flees to the Chaotic Star Sea; 217 Nascent Soul; 885 Spirit Transformation; 1007 enters the Spirit Realm. |
| Spirit Realm | about age 1007-11190 | Age 1007 arrives in the Spirit Realm; 1160 enters the Heavenly Abyss City system; 1420 Void Refinement; 1565 Body Integration; 2468 Great Ascension; 11190 ascends to the Immortal Realm. |
| Immortal World Arc | after about age 11190 | The sequel calendar counts from the Linghuan Realm thread: around year 10 reaches the Immortal Realm, year 20 enters Candle Dragon Dao, around year 660 North Cold conflict, around year 2153 True Word Sect ruins, around year 2156 Gray Realm, and after year 5000 the endgame; roughly read it as age 11190 plus sequel-calendar years. |

References used for this estimate: [Han Li age timeline](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/476520969), [Immortal World Arc calendar](https://www.cnblogs.com/doseoer/p/17834353.html).
