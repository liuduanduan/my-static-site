# Mahayana / Great Vehicle (大乘期, Dàchéng Qī)

<div class="entry-layout"><main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** Mahayana / Great Vehicle belongs to the Middle Realm. Lifespan reference: over one hundred thousand years. Body and power are remade, approaching the peak of mortal-world cultivation.

**中文：** 大乘期属于中境界，寿元参考为十万年以上。肉身与法力重铸，接近凡界修炼顶端。

## Description

**English:** This realm is one step in the fourteen-realm ladder. It changes how cultivators store power, understand the Dao, survive conflict, and interact with the world.

**中文：** 这一境界是十四级修炼阶梯中的关键节点，会改变修士储存力量、理解大道、面对冲突和接触世界的方式。

## Key Characteristics

- **Realm Tier:** Middle Realm / 中境界
- **Lifespan:** over one hundred thousand years
- **Power Shift:** Body and power are remade, approaching the peak of mortal-world cultivation.
- **Narrative Role:** It marks a new threshold of ability, status, and risk.

## Breakthrough Conditions

**English:** Complete a proto-Dao and prepare for the final trial before ascension.

**中文：** 完成大道雏形，准备面对飞升前的终极考验。
