## Description

Foundation Building stabilizes the cultivator's body, meridians, and spiritual base. It is often the first major bottleneck after [Qi Refining](/en/cultivation-system/qi-refining).

## Traits

- Longer lifespan and stronger qi control
- Better access to sect status, missions, and inheritances
- Breakthrough aids such as the [Foundation Establishment Pill](/en/glossary/foundation-establishment-pill)

## Narrative Function

Reaching this realm often changes a character from disposable novice to recognized cultivator.
