# Da Luo / Great Luo (大罗境, Dàluó Jìng)

<div class="entry-layout"><main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** Da Luo / Great Luo belongs to the Upper Realm. Lifespan reference: immortal longevity. Da Luo represents a lofty immortal state less bound by ordinary space-time.

**中文：** 大罗境属于上境界，寿元参考为长生。大罗金仙象征不受普通时空束缚的高位仙境。

## Description

**English:** This realm is one step in the fourteen-realm ladder. It changes how cultivators store power, understand the Dao, survive conflict, and interact with the world.

**中文：** 这一境界是十四级修炼阶梯中的关键节点，会改变修士储存力量、理解大道、面对冲突和接触世界的方式。

## Key Characteristics

- **Realm Tier:** Upper Realm / 上境界
- **Lifespan:** immortal longevity
- **Power Shift:** Da Luo represents a lofty immortal state less bound by ordinary space-time.
- **Narrative Role:** It marks a new threshold of ability, status, and risk.

## Breakthrough Conditions

**English:** Move beyond ordinary immortal limits and form a vast, stable Dao fruit.

**中文：** 跨越普通仙道限制，形成稳定而宏大的道果。
