# 大罗境 · Da Luo (Dàluó Jìng)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 大罗境是上境界的第四个阶段，修仙者成就大罗金仙，距离道祖只有一步之遥。大罗境修士在仙界中是顶尖的存在。

**English:** Da Luo is the fourth realm of the Upper Realm, where cultivators achieve Da Luo Golden Immortal status, standing only one step away from the Dao Ancestor. Da Luo cultivators are the pinnacle of existence in the Immortal Realm.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

大罗境是修仙境界中的第十三个主要境界，位于太乙境之后、道祖境之前。修仙者将玉身进一步提升，达到大罗金仙的境界。大罗境修士在仙界中是顶尖的存在，距离最高境界只有一步之遥。

百度百科中，大罗是道教中的最高位阶之一，大罗金仙代表着仙界中的极致力量。

**English:**

Da Luo is the thirteenth major cultivation realm, situated between Taiyi and the Dao Ancestor. Cultivators further elevate their jade body to reach the Da Luo Golden Immortal realm. Da Luo cultivators are the pinnacle of existence in the Immortal Realm, only one step away from the highest realm.

In Baidu Baike, Da Luo is one of the highest ranks in Taoism, representing the ultimate power in the Immortal Realm.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **大罗金仙**：成就大罗金仙之位
2. **仙界顶尖**：在仙界中位于金字塔顶端
3. **法力无限**：法力接近无穷无尽
4. **万劫不磨**：经历万劫而不灭
5. **半步道祖**：距离道祖只有一步之遥

**English:**

1. **Da Luo Golden Immortal**: Achieving the position of Da Luo Golden Immortal
2. **Immortal Realm Pinnacle**: Sitting at the top of the Immortal Realm pyramid
3. **Infinite Power**: Power is nearly inexhaustible
4. **Impervious to All Tribulations**: Survives all tribulations without perishing
5. **Half-Step Dao Ancestor**: Only one step away from the Dao Ancestor

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从太乙境突破到大罗境需要：
1. 太乙境大圆满
2. 对大道法则的极致理解
3. 经历多次大劫而不灭
4. 极大的机缘和造化
5. 接近天道的感悟

**English:**

Breaking through from Taiyi to Da Luo requires:
1. Great Perfection of Taiyi
2. Ultimate understanding of the laws of the Great Dao
3. Surviving multiple great tribulations
4. Exceptional opportunities and fortune
5. Comprehension approaching the Heavenly Dao

---

## 大罗的地位 · Status of Da Luo

**中文：**

大罗境修士是仙界中最顶尖的存在，地位尊贵无比。大罗金仙在仙界中极其稀少，每一位都是影响仙界格局的大人物。

**English:**

Da Luo cultivators are the most supreme existences in the Immortal Realm, with the most revered status. Da Luo Golden Immortals are extremely rare, and each one is a figure who can shape the order of the Immortal Realm.
