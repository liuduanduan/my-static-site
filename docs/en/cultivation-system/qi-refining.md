# 炼气期 · Qi Refining (Liànqì Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 炼气期是修仙之路的起点，修炼者通过功法感知天地灵气，并将其引入体内，打通全身经脉。共分13层小境界，是为修仙筑基之始。

**English:** Qi Refining is the starting point of the cultivation journey, where practitioners learn to sense spiritual Qi from heaven and earth and guide it into their bodies, opening up their meridians. It consists of 13 sub-levels and marks the beginning of the cultivation path.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

炼气期是修仙境界中的第一个主要境界，位于凡人阶段的终点、筑基期的起点。修炼者在这个阶段通过特定的功法，感知天地间的灵气，并将其引入体内，沿着经脉运行，逐渐改造肉身。

根据百度百科·修仙词条的记载，炼气期共分为13层小境界，每提升一层，体内灵气量和对灵气的掌控力都会显著增强。炼气期的修炼者寿元与凡人无异，但体质已远超常人。

**English:**

Qi Refining is the first major cultivation realm, situated at the end of the mortal stage and the beginning of Foundation Establishment. In this stage, practitioners use specific techniques to sense the spiritual Qi in heaven and earth, draw it into their bodies, and circulate it through their meridians, gradually transforming their physical form.

According to Baidu Baike, Qi Refining consists of 13 sub-levels. With each level advancement, both the quantity of Qi within the body and the practitioner's control over it increase significantly. Cultivators at this stage have lifespans similar to ordinary mortals, though their physical constitutions far exceed those of common people.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **灵气为气态**：体内灵气呈气态运行，分布于全身经脉
2. **五感增强**：视觉、听觉等五感得到显著提升
3. **百病不侵**：一般疾病无法侵害炼气期修士
4. **13层小境界**：从炼气一层到炼气十三层，层层递进

**English:**

1. **Qi as Gas**: Internal Qi exists in gaseous form, flowing through all meridians
2. **Enhanced Senses**: All five senses are significantly improved
3. **Disease Immunity**: Ordinary illnesses cannot affect Qi Refining cultivators
4. **13 Sub-Levels**: From Qi Refining Level 1 to Level 13

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从凡人突破到炼气期需要：
1. 具备灵根（修仙资质）
2. 修炼入门功法，感知灵气
3. 打通第一处经脉（气感）
4. 在师父或功法指引下，将灵气引入丹田

从炼气期突破到筑基期需要：
1. 体内灵气积累达到极致（炼气十三层大圆满）
2. 丹田内灵气充盈，从气态向液态转化
3. 服用筑基丹等辅助丹药可提高成功率
4. 心性修为达到一定程度

**English:**

Breaking through from mortal to Qi Refining requires:
1. Possessing a spiritual root (cultivation talent)
2. Practicing introductory techniques to sense Qi
3. Opening the first meridian (achieving "Qi sensation")
4. Guiding Qi into the dantian under guidance

Breaking through from Qi Refining to Foundation Establishment requires:
1. Maximum Qi accumulation (Level 13 Great Perfection)
2. Transformation of Qi in the dantian from gaseous to liquid state
3. Foundation Establishment Pills to increase success rate
4. Sufficient mental cultivation

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

炼气期修士的寿元与凡人相近，约为100-120岁，不会因修炼而显著延长。无法辟谷，仍需进食五谷杂粮。无法御剑飞行，只能依靠步行或法术轻身。

**English:**

Qi Refining cultivators have lifespans similar to mortals, approximately 100-120 years, with no significant extension from cultivation. They cannot achieve inedia and still require regular food. They cannot ride flying swords and can only rely on walking or light-body techniques.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，主角韩立在炼气期度过了漫长岁月，从炼气一层一步步修炼到炼气十三层，多次面临瓶颈，最终通过莫大夫的功法和机缘成功筑基。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, the protagonist Han Li spends a considerable period at the Qi Refining stage, gradually advancing from Level 1 to Level 13, facing numerous bottlenecks before finally achieving Foundation Establishment through Doctor Mo's techniques and fortunate encounters.
