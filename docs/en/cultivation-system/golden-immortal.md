# Golden Immortal (金仙境, Jīnxiān Jìng)

<div class="entry-layout"><main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** Golden Immortal belongs to the Upper Realm. Lifespan reference: immortal longevity. Golden Immortals embody deeper stability in power and law comprehension.

**中文：** 金仙境属于上境界，寿元参考为长生。金仙不朽，力量和法则理解进一步稳固。

## Description

**English:** This realm is one step in the fourteen-realm ladder. It changes how cultivators store power, understand the Dao, survive conflict, and interact with the world.

**中文：** 这一境界是十四级修炼阶梯中的关键节点，会改变修士储存力量、理解大道、面对冲突和接触世界的方式。

## Key Characteristics

- **Realm Tier:** Upper Realm / 上境界
- **Lifespan:** immortal longevity
- **Power Shift:** Golden Immortals embody deeper stability in power and law comprehension.
- **Narrative Role:** It marks a new threshold of ability, status, and risk.

## Breakthrough Conditions

**English:** Refine immortal body and law foundation into a stable Dao fruit.

**中文：** 凝练仙躯与法则根基，使自身道果更加稳固。
