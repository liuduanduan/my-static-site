# 金丹期 · Golden Core (Jīndān Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 金丹期是修仙者凝结金丹的阶段，是修仙之路的重要分水岭。金丹是灵气高度压缩后形成的固态能量核心，金丹期修士寿元可达500-800岁。

**English:** The Golden Core realm is the stage where cultivators condense a Golden Core, marking a critical milestone in the cultivation journey. The Golden Core is a solid energy core formed by highly compressed Qi, granting cultivators a lifespan of 500-800 years.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

金丹期（又称结丹期）是修仙境界中的第三个主要境界，位于筑基期之后、元婴期之前。修仙者将丹田中液态的灵气进一步压缩，使其结晶化为固态的金丹。

在道教内丹术中，"金丹"是修炼的终极目标之一，象征着"精、气、神"三者的完美融合。百度百科指出，金丹期是修仙者脱胎换骨的关键阶段。

**English:**

The Golden Core realm (also known as Core Formation) is the third major cultivation realm, situated between Foundation Establishment and Nascent Soul. Cultivators further compress the liquid Qi in their dantian, crystallizing it into a solid Golden Core.

In Taoist Inner Alchemy, the "Golden Core" (金丹) represents one of the ultimate goals of cultivation, symbolizing the perfect integration of Essence (精), Qi (气), and Spirit (神). According to Baidu Baike, the Golden Core realm marks a critical transformation stage for cultivators.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元延长**：金丹期修士的寿元大幅延长，可达500-800岁
2. **御剑飞行**：可驾驭飞剑等法宝进行长途飞行
3. **神识增强**：神识范围大幅扩展，可进行远程感知
4. **辟谷**：可长时间不食人间烟火
5. **金丹品质**：金丹分为下品、中品、上品、极品金丹

**English:**

1. **Extended Lifespan**: Golden Core cultivators can live for 500-800 years
2. **Sword Flight**: Can ride flying swords and other artifacts for long-distance travel
3. **Enhanced Divine Sense**: Greatly expanded spiritual perception range
4. **Inedia**: Can go without food for extended periods
5. **Core Quality**: Categorized as Lower, Middle, Upper, and Supreme Grade

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从筑基期突破到金丹期需要：
1. 丹田内灵气积累到极致
2. 强大的心性修为，克服心魔
3. 合适的功法与机缘
4. 往往需要辅助丹药（如结金丹）

**English:**

Breaking through from Foundation Establishment to Golden Core requires:
1. Maximum accumulation of Qi in the dantian
2. Strong mental cultivation to overcome inner demons
3. Suitable techniques and opportunities
4. Often requires auxiliary pills such as Core Formation Pills

---

## 金丹品质与分类 · Core Quality and Classification

**中文：**

金丹的品质直接影响修士的未来成就：

1. **下品金丹**：最低品质，后续修炼困难
2. **中品金丹**：常见品质，可继续修炼至元婴
3. **上品金丹**：高品质，元婴可期
4. **极品金丹**：最高品质，前途无量

**English:**

The quality of the Golden Core directly affects the cultivator's future achievements:

1. **Lower Grade**: Lowest quality, difficult to progress further
2. **Middle Grade**: Common quality, capable of reaching Nascent Soul
3. **Upper Grade**: High quality, promising Nascent Soul
4. **Supreme Grade**: Highest quality, unlimited potential

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

金丹期修士的寿元延长至500-800岁。可辟谷，无需进食。可御剑飞行。可施展高阶法术。金丹是修士的核心，一旦金丹被毁，修为尽失。

**English:**

Golden Core cultivators have a lifespan of 500-800 years. They can achieve inedia, ride flying swords, and cast high-level spells. The Golden Core is the cultivator's core — if it is destroyed, their cultivation is lost.

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，韩立历经千辛万苦，在众多竞争对手中脱颖而出，成功结丹。金丹期的韩立拥有了飞行的能力和更强大的战斗力，真正成为修仙界的重要人物。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Han Li undergoes countless hardships and stands out among numerous competitors to successfully form his Golden Core. As a Golden Core cultivator, Han Li gains the ability to fly and far greater combat power, becoming a significant figure in the cultivation world.
