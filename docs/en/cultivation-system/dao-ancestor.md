# 道祖境 · Dao Ancestor (Dàozǔ Jìng)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 道祖境是修仙的最高境界，修炼者与大道融为一体，成为万道之祖，永恒不灭。道祖是修仙世界的终极存在。

**English:** The Dao Ancestor realm is the ultimate cultivation realm, where the cultivator merges completely with the Great Dao, becoming the ancestor of all paths — eternal and indestructible. The Dao Ancestor is the ultimate existence in the cultivation world.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

道祖境是修仙境界中的第十四个（最高）境界，位于大罗境之上。修仙者将自身完全融入大道之中，成为大道的化身，万道之祖。

百度百科中，道祖是道教中至高无上的存在，代表着修仙的终极目标。道祖境修士已经超越了仙凡的界限，成为宇宙法则的一部分。

**English:**

The Dao Ancestor realm is the fourteenth (and highest) cultivation realm, situated above Da Luo. Cultivators completely merge themselves into the Great Dao, becoming the embodiment of the Dao and the ancestor of all paths.

In Baidu Baike, the Dao Ancestor is the supreme existence in Taoism, representing the ultimate goal of cultivation. Dao Ancestor cultivators have transcended the boundaries of immortality and mortality, becoming part of the laws of the universe.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **永恒不灭**：道祖是永恒的，宇宙不灭则道祖不灭
2. **万道之祖**：所有大道法则的源头
3. **至高无上**：修仙世界的最高存在
4. **法则化身**：道祖本身就是天道法则
5. **创造与毁灭**：一念可创造世界，一念可毁灭万物

**English:**

1. **Eternal and Indestructible**: The Dao Ancestor is eternal — as long as the universe exists, the Dao Ancestor exists
2. **Ancestor of All Paths**: The source of all Dao laws
3. **Supreme Existence**: The highest being in the cultivation world
4. **Law Embodiment**: The Dao Ancestor is the heavenly law itself
5. **Creation and Destruction**: With a single thought, the Dao Ancestor can create worlds or annihilate all things

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从大罗境突破到道祖境需要：
1. 大罗境大圆满
2. 与大道的完全融合
3. 成为大道的化身
4. 理解宇宙的终极奥秘
5. 超越仙凡的界限

**English:**

Breaking through from Da Luo to the Dao Ancestor requires:
1. Great Perfection of Da Luo
2. Complete merging with the Great Dao
3. Becoming the embodiment of the Dao
4. Understanding the ultimate mysteries of the universe
5. Transcending the boundaries of immortality and mortality

---

## 道祖的地位 · Status of Dao Ancestor

**中文：**

道祖是修仙世界的终极存在，一切修道者的终极目标。道祖境修士数量极其稀少，每一位都是传说中的传说。道祖已经超越了"仙"的概念，成为了"道"本身。

**English:**

The Dao Ancestor is the ultimate existence of the cultivation world, the ultimate goal of all cultivators. Dao Ancestors are extremely rare, each one a legend among legends. The Dao Ancestor has transcended the concept of "immortal" and has become the "Dao" itself.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》的后续体系中，道祖是仙界中至高无上的存在。主角韩立的修仙之路，最终的追求便是成就道祖之境。

**English:**

In the expanded universe of *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, the Dao Ancestor is the supreme existence in the Immortal Realm. The ultimate pursuit of the protagonist Han Li's cultivation journey is to achieve the Dao Ancestor realm.
