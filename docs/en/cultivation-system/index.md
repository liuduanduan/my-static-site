# Cultivation System

This overview organizes a fourteen-realm ladder from Qi Refining to Dao Ancestor. It is a practical reference for readers, translators, writers, and game designers.

## Realm Timeline

<div class="realm-timeline">
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/qi-refining"><span class="mark">1</span><span><h3>Qi Refining / 炼气期</h3><p>Lower Realm · 100-120 years · Qi remains gaseous; the focus is sensing Qi and drawing it into the body.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/foundation-building"><span class="mark">2</span><span><h3>Foundation Establishment / 筑基期</h3><p>Lower Realm · about 200 years · Qi condenses from gas into liquid and the cultivator builds a stable foundation.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/core-formation"><span class="mark">3</span><span><h3>Core Formation / Golden Core / 结丹期 / 金丹期</h3><p>Lower Realm · 500-800 years · Liquid spiritual power crystallizes into a solid Golden Core, a major milestone.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/nascent-soul"><span class="mark">4</span><span><h3>Nascent Soul / 元婴期</h3><p>Lower Realm · 1000-2000 years · The Golden Core transforms into a Nascent Soul, greatly strengthening divine sense.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/spirit-transformation"><span class="mark">5</span><span><h3>Spirit Severing / Transformation / 化神期</h3><p>Lower Realm · several thousand years · The Nascent Soul transforms into spirit and begins touching higher laws.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/void-refining"><span class="mark">6</span><span><h3>Void Refinement / 炼虚期</h3><p>Middle Realm · over ten thousand years · Spirit and body develop a deeper relationship with void and world power.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/body-integration"><span class="mark">7</span><span><h3>Unity / Integration / 合体期</h3><p>Middle Realm · tens of thousands of years · Spirit, power, and body integrate, improving combat and survival dramatically.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/great-ascension"><span class="mark">8</span><span><h3>Mahayana / Great Vehicle / 大乘期</h3><p>Middle Realm · over one hundred thousand years · Body and power are remade, approaching the peak of mortal-world cultivation.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/tribulation-crossing"><span class="mark">9</span><span><h3>Tribulation Transcendence / 渡劫期</h3><p>Middle Realm · over one hundred thousand years · Surviving heavenly tribulation opens the path toward the immortal realm.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/true-immortal"><span class="mark">10</span><span><h3>True Immortal / 真仙境</h3><p>Upper Realm · immortal longevity · The initial immortal stage after ascension, marked by immortal power.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/golden-immortal"><span class="mark">11</span><span><h3>Golden Immortal / 金仙境</h3><p>Upper Realm · immortal longevity · Golden Immortals embody deeper stability in power and law comprehension.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/taiyi"><span class="mark">12</span><span><h3>Taiyi / Jade Immortal / 太乙境 / 玉仙境</h3><p>Upper Realm · immortal longevity · A higher immortal stage that approaches broader systems of law.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/da-luo"><span class="mark">13</span><span><h3>Da Luo / Great Luo / 大罗境</h3><p>Upper Realm · immortal longevity · Da Luo represents a lofty immortal state less bound by ordinary space-time.</p></span></a>
<a class="realm-step" href="/en/cultivation-system/dao-ancestor"><span class="mark">14</span><span><h3>Dao Ancestor / 道祖境</h3><p>Upper Realm · eternal · The highest immortal stage, an origin-level existence for countless Daos.</p></span></a>
</div>
