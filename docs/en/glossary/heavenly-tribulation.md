# Tribulation (天劫, Tiānjié)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** A heavenly trial faced during major cultivation breakthroughs.

**中文：** 修炼者突破重大境界时必须面对的天道考验。

## Definition

**English:** Tribulation is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A heavenly trial faced during major cultivation breakthroughs. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 天劫（Tiānjié）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。修炼者突破重大境界时必须面对的天道考验。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** Tribulation turns advancement into a trial of body, mind, karma, and fate.

**中文：** 天劫把修炼从资源积累转化为风险叙事，考验肉身、心境和因果。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
