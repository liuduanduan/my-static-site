# Righteous Cultivator (正道, Zhèngdào)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** A cultivator or faction aligned with orthodox cultivation order.

**中文：** 遵循正统秩序、宗门伦理或主流修炼体系的修士与阵营。

## Definition

**English:** Righteous Cultivator is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A cultivator or faction aligned with orthodox cultivation order. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 正道（Zhèngdào）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。遵循正统秩序、宗门伦理或主流修炼体系的修士与阵营。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** Righteous factions are not always purely good; they often represent order and reputation.

**中文：** 正道并不总等于善，它经常与规则、名望、宗门利益和秩序维护相连。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
