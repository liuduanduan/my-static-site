# Ascension (飞升, Fēishēng)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** The passage from a lower world into a higher realm or immortal world.

**中文：** 修炼大成后进入更高层世界或仙界的过程。

## Definition

**English:** Ascension is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. The passage from a lower world into a higher realm or immortal world. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 飞升（Fēishēng）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。修炼大成后进入更高层世界或仙界的过程。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** Ascension often closes the mortal arc and opens higher-world conflicts.

**中文：** 飞升通常是凡俗修炼线的终点，也是新世界、更高冲突和仙界等级的开端。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
