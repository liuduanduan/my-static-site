# Golden Core (金丹, Jīndān)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** A solid inner core formed during Core Formation and a major cultivation milestone.

**中文：** 结丹期凝结的固态能量核心，也是修仙道路的重要分水岭。

## Definition

**English:** Golden Core is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A solid inner core formed during Core Formation and a major cultivation milestone. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 金丹（Jīndān）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。结丹期凝结的固态能量核心，也是修仙道路的重要分水岭。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** The Golden Core symbolizes a stable condensation of essence, Qi, and spirit.

**中文：** 金丹象征精、气、神的初步凝聚，常意味着修士开始真正脱离凡俗。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
