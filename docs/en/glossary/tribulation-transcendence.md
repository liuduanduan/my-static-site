# Tribulation Transcendence (渡劫, Dùjié)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** The process of surviving heavenly tribulation and crossing a major threshold.

**中文：** 度过天劫、跨越重大境界或飞升门槛的过程。

## Definition

**English:** Tribulation Transcendence is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. The process of surviving heavenly tribulation and crossing a major threshold. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 渡劫（Dùjié）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。度过天劫、跨越重大境界或飞升门槛的过程。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** Tribulation transcendence tests accumulated power, temperament, karma, and protection.

**中文：** 渡劫通常集中展示修士积累、心性、因果和外部护道力量。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
