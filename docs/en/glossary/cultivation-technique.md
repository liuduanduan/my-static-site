# Cultivation Technique (功法, Gōngfǎ)

<div class="entry-layout">
  <main class="entry-main">

## Summary

**English:** A method that guides how cultivators absorb, refine, and circulate power.

**中文：** 引导修士吸纳、炼化、运转力量的具体修炼方法。

## Definition

**English:** Cultivation Technique is a recurring xianxia and cultivation concept. A method that guides how cultivators absorb, refine, and circulate power. The exact rules change from novel to novel, but the term usually helps explain progression, conflict, resources, or cultural imagination.

**中文：** 功法（Gōngfǎ）是修仙叙事中的常见概念。引导修士吸纳、炼化、运转力量的具体修炼方法。不同作品会有各自设定，但它通常用于解释境界进阶、资源分配、修炼风险或文化想象。

## Role in Cultivation

**English:** Techniques shape speed, attributes, breakthroughs, and combat style.

**中文：** 功法决定修炼速度、属性倾向、突破路径和战斗风格。

## In Classic Works

**English:** In works such as *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, *Stellar Travel*, and *Xian Ni*, this concept is usually embedded in a larger system of realms, techniques, sects, and resources.

**中文：** 在《凡人修仙传》《飘邈之旅》《仙逆》等作品中，这一概念通常与境界、功法、宗门和资源体系共同出现，帮助读者理解角色成长逻辑。
