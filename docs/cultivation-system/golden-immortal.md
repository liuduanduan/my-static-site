# 金仙境 · Golden Immortal (Jīnxiān Jìng)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 金仙境是上境界的第二个阶段，真仙之体化为不朽金身，法力达到新的高度。金仙在仙界中拥有更高的地位和更强的实力。

**English:** Golden Immortal is the second realm of the Upper Realm, where the True Immortal body transforms into an indestructible golden body, and power reaches new heights. Golden Immortals hold higher status and greater power in the Immortal Realm.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

金仙境是修仙境界中的第十一个主要境界，位于真仙境之后、太乙境之前。修仙者将真仙之体进一步淬炼，化为不朽金身，法力大增。

百度百科将金仙描述为"仙界之中坚"，金仙在仙界中拥有一定的地位和影响力，是仙界的中坚力量。

**English:**

Golden Immortal is the eleventh major cultivation realm, situated between True Immortal and Taiyi. Cultivators further temper their True Immortal bodies into indestructible golden bodies, greatly increasing their power.

Baidu Baike describes Golden Immortals as the "backbone of the Immortal Realm." They hold a certain status and influence in the Immortal Realm, serving as its main force.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **不朽金身**：肉身化为金身，万劫不磨
2. **法力大增**：法力远超真仙
3. **仙界地位**：金仙在仙界中拥有较高地位
4. **永存不灭**：金仙几乎无法被彻底消灭
5. **仙界中坚**：是仙界的中坚力量

**English:**

1. **Indestructible Golden Body**: The body transforms into a golden body, impervious to tribulations
2. **Greatly Increased Power**: Far surpassing True Immortals
3. **Immortal Realm Status**: Holds higher status in the Immortal Realm
4. **Indestructible**: Almost impossible to completely destroy
5. **Main Force**: The backbone of the Immortal Realm

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从真仙境突破到金仙境需要：
1. 真仙境大圆满
2. 将真仙之体淬炼为金身
3. 对仙界法则的深入理解
4. 长时间的积累和修炼
5. 合适的机缘

**English:**

Breaking through from True Immortal to Golden Immortal requires:
1. Great Perfection of True Immortal
2. Tempering the True Immortal body into a golden body
3. Deep understanding of the Immortal Realm's laws
4. Long-term accumulation and cultivation
5. Suitable opportunities

---

## 金仙的地位 · Status of Golden Immortals

**中文：**

金仙在仙界中拥有中等地位，高于真仙、低于太乙。金仙可以开宗立派，成为一方仙家势力。金仙也是仙界中最活跃的群体，参与仙界事务。

**English:**

Golden Immortals hold middle-level status in the Immortal Realm — above True Immortals and below Taiyi. Golden Immortals can found their own sects and establish immortal forces. They are also the most active group in the Immortal Realm, participating in its affairs.
