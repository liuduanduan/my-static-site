# 渡劫期 · Tribulation Transcendence (Dùjié Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 渡劫期是修仙者飞升前的最后一个阶段，修仙者必须度过天劫的考验才能飞升仙界成为真正的仙人。渡劫失败则形神俱灭。

**English:** Tribulation Transcendence is the final stage before ascension. Cultivators must survive the heavenly tribulation to ascend to the Immortal Realm and become true immortals. Failure means complete annihilation of both body and soul.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

渡劫期是修仙境界中的第九个主要境界，位于大乘期之后、真仙境之前。修仙者在此阶段需要面对天劫的考验，度过天劫后飞升仙界，成为真仙。

百度百科记载，渡劫是修仙者面临的最大考验，也是最大的机遇。成功则一步登天，失败则灰飞烟灭。

**English:**

Tribulation Transcendence is the ninth major cultivation realm, situated between Mahayana and True Immortal. Cultivators at this stage must face the test of the heavenly tribulation. Upon surviving, they ascend to the Immortal Realm and become True Immortals.

According to Baidu Baike, tribulation is the greatest test and also the greatest opportunity for cultivators. Success leads to immortality — failure leads to utter destruction.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元极长**：渡劫期修士寿元可达十万年以上
2. **天劫考验**：必须经历天劫（雷劫、心劫等）
3. **一步登仙**：度过天劫即飞升仙界
4. **形神俱灭**：渡劫失败则彻底消亡
5. **天地感应**：天劫降临时天地异象

**English:**

1. **Extremely Long Lifespan**: Tribulation Transcendence cultivators can live for over 100,000 years
2. **Tribulation Test**: Must undergo the heavenly tribulation (thunder tribulation, heart tribulation, etc.)
3. **One Step to Immortality**: Surviving means ascension to the Immortal Realm
4. **Complete Annihilation**: Failure means complete destruction
5. **Heavenly Response**: Tribulation is accompanied by celestial phenomena

---

## 天劫的种类 · Types of Tribulation

**中文：**

1. **雷劫**：天地降下天雷，以雷电之力考验
2. **心劫**：考验修炼者的心性，内心产生幻境
3. **风劫**：以罡风考验，吹散元神
4. **火劫**：以真火考验，焚烧肉身
5. **天劫**：综合以上多种劫难

**English:**

1. **Thunder Tribulation**: Heaven sends down heavenly thunder to test the cultivator
2. **Heart Tribulation**: Tests the cultivator's mental state through illusions
3. **Wind Tribulation**: Uses wind to test and scatter the primordial spirit
4. **Fire Tribulation**: Uses true fire to test and burn the physical body
5. **Heavenly Tribulation**: A combination of the above

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从大乘期突破到渡劫期（飞升）需要：
1. 大乘期大圆满
2. 引动天劫
3. 拥有渡劫的手段和法宝
4. 强大的心性修为
5. 天时地利人和的机缘

**English:**

Breaking through from Mahayana to Tribulation Transcendence (ascension) requires:
1. Great Perfection of Mahayana
2. Triggering the heavenly tribulation
3. Possessing tools and artifacts to survive the tribulation
4. Extremely strong mental cultivation
5. The perfect timing and opportunity

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

渡劫期修士的寿元理论上可达十万年以上，但天劫随时可能降临，渡劫成功后飞升仙界，渡劫失败则彻底消亡。

**English:**

Tribulation Transcendence cultivators theoretically have a lifespan of over 100,000 years, but the tribulation can descend at any time. Success leads to ascension — failure leads to complete destruction.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，韩立经历了多次天劫，每次渡劫都是九死一生。渡劫是修仙路上最艰难的一关，也是修仙者最终能否飞升的关键。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Han Li undergoes multiple tribulations, each a life-and-death ordeal. Tribulation is the most difficult challenge on the cultivation path and the key to whether a cultivator can ultimately ascend.
