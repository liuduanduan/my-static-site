# 合体期 · Unity (Hétǐ Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 合体期是中境界的第二个阶段，元婴虚影与肉身完美融合，达到"合体"之境。合体期修士的寿元可达数万年，法力与肉身合而为一。

**English:** Unity is the second realm of the Middle Realm, where the void shadow and the physical body perfectly merge into one. Unity cultivators can live for tens of thousands of years, with power and body unified into a single entity.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

合体期是修仙境界中的第七个主要境界，位于炼虚期之后、大乘期之前。修仙者将炼虚期的虚影重新与肉身结合，达到"合体"的境界。此时修士的法力、元神、肉身三者完美合一，不再有分离。

百度百科记载，合体期修士的强大在于他们的完整性——元神与肉身不再有隔阂。

**English:**

Unity is the seventh major cultivation realm, situated between Void Refinement and Mahayana. Cultivators reunite the void shadow with their physical body, achieving a state of "Unity." At this point, the cultivator's power, spirit, and body are perfectly integrated with no separation.

According to Baidu Baike, the power of Unity cultivators lies in their completeness — there is no longer any separation between spirit and body.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元延长**：合体期修士寿元可达数万年
2. **身心合一**：法力、元神、肉身完美融合
3. **实力飞跃**：战斗力远超炼虚期修士
4. **肉身成圣**：肉身达到极高的强度
5. **中境界中期**：处于中境界的中心阶段

**English:**

1. **Extended Lifespan**: Unity cultivators can live for tens of thousands of years
2. **Body-Mind Unity**: Power, spirit, and body perfectly integrated
3. **Power Leap**: Combat ability far surpasses Void Refinement cultivators
4. **Sainted Body**: Physical body reaches an extremely high level of strength
5. **Middle Realm Mid-Stage**: Positioned at the center of the Middle Realm

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从炼虚期突破到合体期需要：
1. 炼虚期大圆满
2. 虚影与肉身的重新融合
3. 对天地法则的深入理解
4. 经历"合体之劫"
5. 强大的心性修为

**English:**

Breaking through from Void Refinement to Unity requires:
1. Great Perfection of Void Refinement
2. Reintegration of the void shadow with the physical body
3. Deep understanding of heavenly laws
4. Undergoing the "Unity Tribulation"
5. Strong mental cultivation

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

合体期修士的寿元延长至数万年。合体期修士的肉身与元神合一，不再惧怕元婴离体带来的风险。合体期修士的目标是达到大乘期。

**English:**

Unity cultivators have a lifespan of tens of thousands of years. With body and spirit unified, they no longer fear the risks of Nascent Soul departure. Their goal is to reach the Mahayana realm.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，合体期修士在灵界中已经是顶尖的存在，可以开宗立派，成为一方霸主。合体期的韩立已经具备了撼动灵界格局的实力。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Unity cultivators are already among the top existences in the Spirit Realm, capable of founding sects and becoming regional powers. Han Li at the Unity stage possesses the strength to reshape the Spirit Realm's order.
