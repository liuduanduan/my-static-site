# 炼虚期 · Void Refinement (Liànxū Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 炼虚期是中境界的第一个阶段，元婴化为虚影，融入天地之中。炼虚期修士的寿元可达上万年，开始真正触摸天道的法则。

**English:** Void Refinement is the first realm of the Middle Realm, where the Nascent Soul transforms into a void shadow and merges with the heavens. Void Refinement cultivators can live for tens of thousands of years and begin to truly grasp the laws of the Heavenly Dao.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

炼虚期是修仙境界中的第六个主要境界，位于化神期之后、合体期之前。修仙者将化神期的修为进一步凝练，使元婴化为虚影，融入天地虚空之中。此时修士不再是单纯的"修炼"，而是开始与天地"同化"。

百度百科将炼虚期归为中境界的起始阶段，标志着修仙者开始探索天地的终极奥秘。

**English:**

Void Refinement is the sixth major cultivation realm, situated between Spirit Severing and Unity. Cultivators further refine their Spirit Severing cultivation, transforming their Nascent Soul into a void shadow that merges with the emptiness of heaven and earth. At this stage, cultivators are no longer simply "cultivating" but beginning to "assimilate" with the heavens.

According to Baidu Baike, Void Refinement marks the beginning of the Middle Realm, where cultivators begin to explore the ultimate mysteries of heaven and earth.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元大幅延长**：炼虚期修士寿元可达上万年
2. **元婴化虚**：元婴化作虚影，无形无质
3. **天地同化**：开始与天地融为一体
4. **法则感悟**：深刻感悟天地法则
5. **中境界之始**：进入全新的修炼阶段

**English:**

1. **Greatly Extended Lifespan**: Void Refinement cultivators can live for tens of thousands of years
2. **Void Nascent Soul**: The Nascent Soul becomes a void shadow — formless and intangible
3. **Heavenly Assimilation**: Beginning to merge with the heavens
4. **Law Comprehension**: Deep understanding of heavenly laws
5. **Middle Realm Beginning**: Entering a completely new cultivation phase

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从化神期突破到炼虚期需要：
1. 化神期大圆满
2. 对天地法则的深刻感悟
3. 将元婴转化为虚影
4. 经历"虚空之劫"
5. 需要大量的机缘和时间的沉淀

**English:**

Breaking through from Spirit Severing to Void Refinement requires:
1. Great Perfection of Spirit Severing
2. Deep comprehension of heavenly laws
3. Transforming the Nascent Soul into a void shadow
4. Undergoing the "Void Tribulation"
5. Extensive opportunities and the passage of time

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

炼虚期修士的寿元延长至上万年。炼虚期修士不再受凡俗的限制，可以在虚空中穿梭。炼虚期修士的目标是达到合体期，这需要更深的感悟。

**English:**

Void Refinement cultivators have a lifespan of tens of thousands of years. They are no longer bound by mortal limitations and can traverse through the void. Their goal is to reach the Unity realm, which requires even deeper comprehension.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，炼虚期是韩立进入灵界后面临的第一个大境界。炼虚期修士在灵界中已是重要人物，可以参与更大的世界格局。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Void Refinement is the first major realm Han Li faces after entering the Spirit Realm. Void Refinement cultivators are already significant figures in the Spirit Realm, capable of participating in the broader world order.
