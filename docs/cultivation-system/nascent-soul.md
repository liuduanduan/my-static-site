# 元婴期 · Nascent Soul (Yuányīng Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 元婴期是修仙者金丹化为元婴的阶段，元婴是金丹和元神合二为一的产物，可离体遨游，是修仙之路的重大飞跃。寿元可达1000-2000岁。

**English:** The Nascent Soul realm is the stage where the Golden Core transforms into a Nascent Soul — the fusion of the Golden Core and the primordial spirit. The Nascent Soul can leave the physical body, marking a major leap in the cultivation journey. Lifespan reaches 1000-2000 years.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

元婴期是修仙境界中的第四个主要境界，位于金丹期之后、化神期之前。修仙者将金丹与自身的元神合二为一，在金丹内部孕育出"元婴"——一个与修炼者面貌相同的微型本尊。

根据百度百科的记载，元婴期是修仙者"脱胎换骨"的关键节点。元婴期修士不仅拥有强大的法力，更重要的是，即使肉身被毁，元婴依然可以存活并寻找新的肉身，在某种意义上实现了"不死"。

**English:**

The Nascent Soul realm is the fourth major cultivation realm, situated between the Golden Core and Spirit Severing realms. Cultivators merge their Golden Core with their primordial spirit, giving birth to a "Nascent Soul" — a miniature avatar that resembles the cultivator.

According to Baidu Baike, the Nascent Soul realm is a critical "rebirth" node for cultivators. Nascent Soul cultivators possess immense power and, more importantly, even if their physical body is destroyed, their Nascent Soul can survive and seek a new body, achieving a form of "immortality."

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元延长**：元婴期修士寿元可达1000-2000岁
2. **元婴离体**：元婴可脱离肉身独立存在，可飞行游历
3. **夺舍重生**：元婴可占据他人肉身（夺舍）
4. **法力大增**：法力远超金丹期，可施展大规模法术
5. **元婴品质**：元婴也分品质，越强则潜力越大

**English:**

1. **Extended Lifespan**: Nascent Soul cultivators can live for 1000-2000 years
2. **Soul Departure**: The Nascent Soul can leave the physical body and travel independently
3. **Body Possession**: The Nascent Soul can occupy another's body (夺舍, Duóshè)
4. **Massive Power Increase**: Far more powerful than Golden Core cultivators, capable of large-scale spells
5. **Soul Quality**: Nascent Souls also vary in quality, affecting future potential

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从金丹期突破到元婴期需要：
1. 金丹达到圆满状态
2. 金丹内"孕育"出元神的雏形
3. 经历"金丹化婴"的关键过程
4. 强大的心性修为，否则可能走火入魔
5. 往往需要特殊机缘或丹药辅助（如元婴丹）

**English:**

Breaking through from Golden Core to Nascent Soul requires:
1. The Golden Core reaching perfection
2. Cultivating the embryonic form of the primordial spirit within the Golden Core
3. Undergoing the critical process of "Golden Core to Infant Transformation"
4. Strong mental cultivation to avoid Qi Deviation
5. Often requires special opportunities or pills (such as Nascent Soul Pills)

---

## 夺舍机制 · Body Possession Mechanism

**中文：**

元婴期修士最大的优势是拥有"夺舍"的能力：
- 元婴出窍后可以寻找新的身体
- 夺舍后能够快速恢复修为
- 每次夺舍会消耗一定的元神力量
- 夺舍存在风险，可能被抵抗

**English:**

The greatest advantage of Nascent Soul cultivators is the ability of "body possession":
- The Nascent Soul can leave the body and search for a new host
- Cultivation can be quickly restored after possession
- Each possession consumes some of the soul's power
- Possession carries risks and may be resisted

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

元婴期修士的寿元延长至1000-2000岁。元婴离体后，肉身处于休眠状态。元婴不可长时间离体，否则肉身会死亡。元婴被毁则修士彻底消亡。

**English:**

Nascent Soul cultivators have a lifespan of 1000-2000 years. When the Nascent Soul departs, the physical body enters a dormant state. The Nascent Soul cannot remain away from the body for extended periods, or the body will die. If the Nascent Soul is destroyed, the cultivator perishes completely.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，韩立通过金丹突破至元婴期，实力发生了质的飞跃。元婴期的他可以元婴出窍，探索危险的禁地，即使在险境中也能保留一线生机。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Han Li breaks through from Golden Core to Nascent Soul, achieving a qualitative leap in power. As a Nascent Soul cultivator, he can send his Nascent Soul out of his body to explore dangerous forbidden areas, preserving a chance of survival even in perilous situations.
