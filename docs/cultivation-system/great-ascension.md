# 大乘期 · Mahayana (Dàchéng Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 大乘期是中境界的巅峰，修仙者在此阶段肉身重铸，法力达到前所未有的高度。寿元可达十万年以上，是人间界的巅峰存在。

**English:** Mahayana is the peak of the Middle Realm, where cultivators reforge their physical bodies and reach unprecedented levels of power. Lifespan can exceed one hundred thousand years, making them the pinnacle of existence in the mortal realm.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

大乘期是修仙境界中的第八个主要境界，位于合体期之后、渡劫期之前。修仙者在此阶段将肉身进行全面的重铸，肉身强度达到极致，法力浩瀚无边。

百度百科将大乘期描述为"中境界的顶点"，大乘期修士距离仙道只有一步之遥，但这一步需要经历天劫的考验。

**English:**

Mahayana is the eighth major cultivation realm, situated between Unity and Tribulation Transcendence. Cultivators at this stage completely reforge their physical bodies, reaching the ultimate level of physical strength with boundless power.

Baidu Baike describes Mahayana as the "peak of the Middle Realm." Mahayana cultivators are only one step away from immortality — but that step requires passing through the tribulation.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元极长**：大乘期修士寿元可达十万年以上
2. **肉身重铸**：肉身达到极致的强度
3. **法力无边**：法力浩瀚，堪比仙人
4. **法则领悟**：对天地法则的理解极为深刻
5. **一步登仙**：渡劫后即可飞升仙界

**English:**

1. **Extremely Long Lifespan**: Mahayana cultivators can live for over 100,000 years
2. **Body Reforging**: Physical body reaches ultimate strength
3. **Boundless Power**: Power is vast, comparable to immortals
4. **Law Mastery**: Extremely deep understanding of heavenly laws
5. **One Step to Immortality**: After tribulation, they can ascend to the Immortal Realm

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从合体期突破到大乘期需要：
1. 合体期大圆满
2. 肉身的全面重铸
3. 对天道的深刻感悟
4. 经历"大乘之劫"
5. 需要海量的资源支持

**English:**

Breaking through from Unity to Mahayana requires:
1. Great Perfection of Unity
2. Complete re forging of the physical body
3. Deep comprehension of the Heavenly Dao
4. Undergoing the "Mahayana Tribulation"
5. Massive resources and support

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

大乘期修士的寿元延长至十万年以上。大乘期修士已经站在了人间界的巅峰，他们的目标只有一个——渡劫飞升。

**English:**

Mahayana cultivators have a lifespan of over 100,000 years. They stand at the peak of the mortal realm with only one goal remaining — to transcend tribulation and ascend.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，大乘期的修士在人界和灵界都是传说中的存在。韩立达到大乘期后，已经可以挑战最强大的对手，距离仙道只有一步之遥。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Mahayana cultivators are legendary figures in both the mortal realm and the Spirit Realm. After reaching Mahayana, Han Li can challenge the most powerful opponents, standing only one step away from the immortal path.
