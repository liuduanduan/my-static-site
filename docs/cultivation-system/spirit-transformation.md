# 化神期 · Spirit Severing (Huàshén Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 化神期是修仙者元婴化神的阶段，元婴与天地融为一体，是下境界的巅峰。化神期修士寿元可达数千岁，法力通神。

**English:** Spirit Severing is the stage where the Nascent Soul transforms into Spirit, merging with the heavens and earth. It is the peak of the Lower Realm, granting cultivators a lifespan of thousands of years and god-like power.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

化神期是修仙境界中的第五个主要境界，位于元婴期之后、炼虚期之前。修仙者将元婴进一步升华，使其与天地融为一体，达到"化神"的境界。化神期修士的神识覆盖范围极广，可以感知一片区域内的所有动静。

百度百科记载，化神是下境界的最后一个境界，跨过此境便进入中境界。化神期的修炼者已经开始触及"天道"的奥秘。

**English:**

Spirit Severing is the fifth major cultivation realm, situated between Nascent Soul and Void Refinement. Cultivators further elevate their Nascent Soul, merging it with the heavens and earth to achieve the state of "Spirit Transformation." Spirit Severing cultivators have an extremely wide range of divine sense, capable of perceiving all activity within a large area.

According to Baidu Baike, Spirit Severing is the final realm of the Lower Realm — crossing this threshold leads to the Middle Realm. Spirit Severing cultivators have begun to touch the mysteries of the "Heavenly Dao."

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **寿元大幅延长**：化神期修士寿元可达数千岁
2. **神识覆盖**：神识可覆盖一整个大陆
3. **天人合一**：初步感受天地法则，与天地共鸣
4. **神通广大**：可施展呼风唤雨等神通
5. **元婴化神**：元婴已完全与元神融合

**English:**

1. **Greatly Extended Lifespan**: Spirit Severing cultivators can live for thousands of years
2. **Divine Sense Coverage**: Divine sense can cover an entire continent
3. **Unity of Heaven and Man**: Preliminarily sensing the laws of heaven and earth
4. **Great Powers**: Capable of summoning wind and rain and other divine abilities
5. **Soul Transformation**: The Nascent Soul is fully integrated with the primordial spirit

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从元婴期突破到化神期需要：
1. 元婴达到巅峰状态
2. 对天地法则有深刻感悟
3. 经历"化神劫"（天劫的一种）
4. 心性达到"无我"境界
5. 需要大量灵气积累

**English:**

Breaking through from Nascent Soul to Spirit Severing requires:
1. The Nascent Soul reaching its peak state
2. Deep comprehension of the laws of heaven and earth
3. Undergoing the "Spirit Transformation Tribulation"
4. Achieving the mental state of "no-self"
5. Massive accumulation of spiritual Qi

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

化神期修士的寿元延长至数千岁。化神期修士已可完全辟谷，无需食物和水。化神期修士的目标是突破到中境界，这需要更大的机缘和更深的感悟。

**English:**

Spirit Severing cultivators have a lifespan of thousands of years. They have completely achieved inedia and require no food or water. Their goal is to break through to the Middle Realm, which requires even greater opportunities and deeper comprehension.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，化神期是下境界的顶点，韩立在化神期时已经可以纵横人界，罕有敌手。化神期修士在人界被视为传说中的存在。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Spirit Severing is the peak of the Lower Realm. At this stage, Han Li can traverse the mortal world with few rivals, and Spirit Severing cultivators are regarded as legendary figures in the mortal realm.
