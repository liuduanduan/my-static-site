## Description

Spirit Transformation deepens the cultivator's control over spirit, perception, and higher forms of qi. It often begins the shift from local power to world-level influence.

## Traits

- Powerful divine sense and long-range perception
- Stronger resonance with laws, domains, or Dao intent
- Increased importance of mental state and heart demons

## Narrative Function

The story often moves from sect rivalry toward ancient ruins, secret realms, and old monsters at this stage.
