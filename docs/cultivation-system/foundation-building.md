# 筑基期 · Foundation Establishment (Zhùjī Qī)

## 摘要 · Summary

**中文：** 筑基期是修仙的第二个境界，修炼者丹田内的灵气从气态转化为液态，寿元延长至两百岁，真正踏入了修仙的大门。

**English:** Foundation Establishment is the second cultivation realm, where the Qi in the cultivator's dantian transforms from gaseous to liquid state, extending their lifespan to two hundred years and marking their true entry into the world of cultivation.

---

## 定义 · Definition

**中文：**

筑基期是修仙境界中的第二个主要境界，位于炼气期之后、金丹期之前。修炼者将丹田内积累到极致的灵气进行压缩，使其由气态转化为液态，形成"灵液"，这就是"筑基"的本质。

百度百科指出，筑基来源于道教修炼中的"百日筑基"，是修炼者奠定仙道根基的关键阶段。筑基完成后，修炼者便真正拥有了超越凡人的力量。

**English:**

Foundation Establishment is the second major cultivation realm, situated between Qi Refining and the Golden Core realm. Practitioners compress the Qi that has accumulated to its peak in their dantian, transforming it from gaseous to liquid state, forming "spiritual liquid" — this is the essence of Foundation Establishment.

According to Baidu Baike, Foundation Establishment originates from the Taoist concept of "Hundred Days of Foundation Building," a critical stage where cultivators lay the foundation for their immortal path. Upon completion, cultivators truly possess power beyond that of ordinary mortals.

---

## 核心特征 · Key Characteristics

**中文：**

1. **灵气化为液态**：丹田内灵气从气态转化为液态
2. **寿元延长**：筑基期修士寿元延长至两百岁
3. **神识初现**：开始拥有神识，可感知周围环境
4. **法术精通**：可以修炼更高阶的法术
5. **筑基品质**：筑基分为"筑基丹筑基"和"无瑕筑基"等不同品质

**English:**

1. **Liquid Qi**: Qi in the dantian transforms from gaseous to liquid state
2. **Extended Lifespan**: Lifespan extends to approximately 200 years
3. **Divine Sense Emerges**: Begins to develop divine sense to perceive surroundings
4. **Spell Mastery**: Capable of cultivating higher-level spells
5. **Foundation Quality**: Differs in quality—"Pill Foundation" vs. "Flawless Foundation"

---

## 突破条件 · Breakthrough Conditions

**中文：**

从炼气期突破到筑基期需要：
1. 炼气期大圆满（炼气十三层巅峰）
2. 丹田灵气积累到极致，达到饱和状态
3. 服用筑基丹（可大幅提高成功率，但可能影响筑基品质）
4. 有经验的师长护法
5. 心性坚定，克服心魔

**English:**

Breaking through from Qi Refining to Foundation Establishment requires:
1. Great Perfection of Qi Refining (Level 13 peak)
2. Maximum Qi accumulation in the dantian reaching saturation
3. Foundation Establishment Pills (greatly increase success rate but may affect quality)
4. Guidance and protection from experienced masters
5. Firm will to overcome inner demons

---

## 筑基品质 · Foundation Quality

**中文：**

筑基的品质直接影响修士的修仙上限：

1. **筑基丹筑基**：服用筑基丹突破，成功率较高，但品质较低
2. **无瑕筑基**：不借助丹药自行突破，品质最高，潜力最大
3. **有瑕筑基**：筑基过程中出现问题，品质有缺陷，影响后续修炼

**English:**

The quality of Foundation Establishment directly affects a cultivator's upper limit:

1. **Pill Foundation**: Breaking through with Foundation Establishment Pills — higher success rate but lower quality
2. **Flawless Foundation**: Breaking through without pills — highest quality and greatest potential
3. **Flawed Foundation**: Problems during breakthrough — defect in quality, affecting future cultivation

---

## 寿元与限制 · Lifespan and Limitations

**中文：**

筑基期修士的寿元延长至两百岁左右。可以短时间辟谷，但仍需定期进食。可以短距离御物飞行（如驾驭飞剑），但无法长途飞行。

**English:**

Foundation Establishment cultivators have a lifespan of approximately 200 years. They can achieve short-term inedia but still require regular meals. They can perform short-distance object flight (such as riding a flying sword) but cannot travel long distances.

---

## 在经典作品中的体现 · In Classic Works

**中文：**

在《凡人修仙传》中，韩立在炼气期滞留多年，最终通过莫大夫留下的筑基丹成功筑基。筑基后，他的实力大幅提升，正式踏入了修仙界的大门。

**English:**

In *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality*, Han Li lingers at Qi Refining for years before finally achieving Foundation Establishment with Doctor Mo's Foundation Establishment Pill. After Foundation Establishment, his power increases significantly, and he officially steps into the world of cultivation.
