## Description

Core Formation is the stage where refined energy forms a [Golden Core](/glossary/golden-core). The cultivator becomes more self-sufficient and harder to kill.

## Traits

- Large increase in combat power and lifespan
- Core quality may affect future advancement
- Stronger artifacts and sect authority become available

## Narrative Function

Core Formation often marks the first true arrival of a protagonist as a regional power.
