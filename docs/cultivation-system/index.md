# Cultivation System

Xianxia realm systems vary by author, but many stories use a ladder that turns mortal training into spiritual transformation, cosmic trials, and eventual immortality.

## Special Paths

- Loose immortals or scattered immortals may survive failed ascension in some settings.
- Earth immortals, heavenly immortals, and true immortals can appear as later subdivisions.
- Body cultivation, sword cultivation, demonic cultivation, and alchemy-heavy paths may reorder the usual priorities.

## Reading Note

Use this sequence as a translation aid, not a universal canon. Xianxia authors often tune realm names to the needs of their worldbuilding.
